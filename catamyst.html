Catamyst is an action roguelike game designed around creating and throwing potions at hordes of molecular enemies, in a fusion of science and magic!

You play as Willow - a cat witch who has a powerful cauldron bag that can produce potions for them on the fly, whilst learning how to power them up with use.

Along the way you'll unlock powerful enchantments that upgrade your capabilities to take on tougher waves. Catamyst boasts endless replayability with run modifiers and powerful artifacts to take on each run.

Catamyst released on August 19th 2024 via Steam!


- Code, pixel art and project management by myself.
