Hello! I'm Winter / Moss (they/them), a gameplay programmer and indie game developer based in Scotland.

I'm studying Computer Games Development at the University of West Scotland, near Glasgow (4th Year). Currently I'm working on my Fishing/Action game Spirit of the Sea alongside writing my dissertation.

Recently (August 2024), I developed and released my first indie game, Catamyst, an action roguelike. This project was a collaboration with musician and audio designer Cochu. This project was a huge labour of love and I highly recommend taking a look!

This website was made by myself! Please take your time to browse all my works, and feel free to contact me if you need to!
